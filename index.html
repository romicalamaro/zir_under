about

Under is a modular headscarf brand that expresses solidarity and support for Iranian women. Its designs are based on a visual sign language that translates emotional states into graphic patterns, giving voice to women for whom Iran is part of their identity.
All designs on this website were created by Iranian women living outside Iran.
